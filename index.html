Penguins are going Skiing

Due to climate change, penguins have decided to take a cool break and come skiing in our ski resorts. They have a preference for russian, norwegian, islandic and canadian slopes. Nordica seems to be their preferred ski brand, as it is easier for them to carve with their small feet. Poor chaps. We wish you a lot of fun!
